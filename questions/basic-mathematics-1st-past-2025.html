18.

285 watch channel A, 195 watch channel B, 115 watch channel C, 70 watch channel A and B, 50 watch channel B and C, 45 watch channel A and C. If 150 of the viewers watch none of the channels, find how many of them watch:

a. at least one of the channels

b. all three channels

c. two channels only

d. one channel only
